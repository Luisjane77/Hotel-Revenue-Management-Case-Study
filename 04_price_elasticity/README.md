# Price Elasticity and Demand Behavior

## Module Objective

This module analyzes the relationship between ADR and demand in hotel booking data.

The objective is to evaluate how demand responds to ADR changes and whether price sensitivity differs across seasons or customer segments.

---

## Business Questions

- Does demand decrease when ADR increases?
- Are some segments more sensitive to price changes than others?
- Does price sensitivity change by season?
- Where does the hotel appear to have stronger pricing power?

---

## Key Metrics

- ADR
- Room nights
- Revenue
- Price elasticity
- Segment behavior
- Seasonality

---

## Revenue Management Relevance

Price elasticity analysis helps evaluate how much pricing flexibility the hotel may have across different segments and periods.

This supports Revenue Management decisions related to rate increases, seasonal pricing, segment restrictions and inventory protection.
