# Segment Performance Analysis

## Module Objective

This module analyzes hotel customer segments to understand how each segment contributes to room nights, revenue and ADR.

The objective is to identify which segments generate the strongest revenue value and which segments may contribute volume without proportional revenue performance.

---

## Business Questions

- Which segments generate the highest revenue contribution?
- Which segments generate the highest ADR?
- Which segments contribute more room nights than revenue?
- Which segments should be prioritized from a Revenue Management perspective?

---

## Key Metrics

- Room nights
- Revenue
- ADR
- Segment contribution
- Segment value comparison

---

## Revenue Management Relevance

This analysis helps evaluate segment mix and identify where the hotel may be relying on high-volume segments that do not necessarily generate proportional revenue value.

It supports decisions related to segment prioritization, pricing strategy and commercial focus.
