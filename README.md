# Hotel Revenue Management Case Study

This repository presents an end-to-end hotel revenue management case study focused on segmentation, cancellation risk, demand forecasting and pricing strategy.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate how hotel booking data can be transformed into practical Revenue Management insights and business recommendations.

## Project Modules

1. Segment Performance Analysis  
2. Cancellation Risk Analysis  
3. Demand Forecasting by Segment and Month  
4. Price Elasticity and Demand Behavior  
5. Revenue Management Strategy Recommendations  

## Objective

To analyze hotel booking data from a Revenue Management perspective and identify opportunities to improve revenue performance through better segmentation, pricing, demand forecasting and cancellation management.
