# Demand Forecasting by Segment and Month

## Module Objective

This module focuses on forecasting hotel demand by month and customer segment.

The objective is to understand expected demand patterns and support pricing, inventory and commercial decisions.

---

## Business Questions

- How does demand behave across different months?
- Which segments show stronger seasonal patterns?
- How can future demand estimates support pricing decisions?
- Which segments should be monitored more closely during high-demand or low-demand periods?

---

## Key Metrics

- Monthly room nights
- Segment demand
- Forecasted demand
- Seasonality
- Demand trend

---

## Revenue Management Relevance

Demand forecasting is central to Revenue Management because pricing and inventory decisions depend on expected demand.

By forecasting demand by segment, the hotel can better anticipate occupancy pressure, adjust pricing strategy and protect inventory during high-demand periods.
