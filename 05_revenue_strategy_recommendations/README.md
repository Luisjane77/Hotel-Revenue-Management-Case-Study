# Revenue Management Strategy Recommendations

## Module Objective

This module summarizes the main findings from the full case study and translates them into practical Revenue Management recommendations.

The objective is to connect the analytical modules with business decisions related to pricing, segmentation, cancellation management, forecasting and inventory strategy.

---

## Strategic Areas Covered

- Segment prioritization
- Channel mix optimization
- Cancellation risk management
- Forecast-based pricing
- Price elasticity interpretation
- Inventory protection

---

## Revenue Management Perspective

The purpose of this module is to move from analysis to action.

Each recommendation should be linked to a business finding and should explain how the hotel could use the insight in a real Revenue Management context.

---

## Files

This folder will contain the final strategic recommendations based on the previous analytical modules.
