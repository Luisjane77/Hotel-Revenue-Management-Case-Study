# Cancellation Risk Analysis

## Module Objective

This module analyzes booking cancellations and their impact on hotel revenue performance.

The objective is to understand how cancellation behavior affects net revenue, forecast accuracy, channel reliability and potential overbooking decisions.

---

## Business Questions

- Which segments or channels generate higher cancellation risk?
- How do cancellations affect net revenue?
- How does lead time influence cancellation behavior?
- How can cancellation analysis support overbooking and inventory decisions?

---

## Key Metrics

- Cancellation rate
- Revenue lost due to cancellations
- Net revenue
- Lead time
- Channel risk
- Segment risk

---

## Revenue Management Relevance

Cancellations directly affect forecast accuracy, inventory availability and final revenue performance.

Understanding cancellation behavior allows Revenue Management teams to adjust overbooking levels, review cancellation policies and evaluate channel reliability.
