# Executive Summary

## Purpose

This folder contains a concise business-oriented summary of the project for recruiters, hiring managers and Revenue Management professionals.

The objective is to present the case study in a clear and accessible way, without requiring the reader to review every notebook in detail.

---

## Intended Audience

- HR recruiters
- Revenue Managers
- Hiring managers
- Hospitality analytics professionals

---

## Summary Focus

The executive summary will highlight:

- Business problem
- Methodology
- Key findings
- Revenue Management recommendations
- Tools used
- Link to technical modules
